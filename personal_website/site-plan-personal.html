Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help provide handyman services to individuals who need housing services like, plumbing, electricity, furniture making and regular maintainance of household machines.

Audience

The target audience are everyone and anyone who face problems concerning the flow of water and fluids in their building (plumbing), problems with the electrical connections in their bulidings (electricity), in need of furnitures or fixing furnitures in their building (furniture making) and maintaining of machines e.g generators, washing machines and/or refrigerators. We provide services to households, companies, factories, schools, banks and hospitals.
